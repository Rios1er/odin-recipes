Spaghetti

Description

It's name was officially introduced in 1874 and originated in Sicily, Italy. You're taste buds wont thank you enough when you try this savory dish!


Ingredients
  • Noodle
  • beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Herbs
  • Meatballs
  • Cheese
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Instructions
  1. bowl large pot of water
  2. Once water has boiled, add noddles into pot and turn heat down to med/high.
  3. While noodles are boiling, start prepping beef/meatballs.
  4. Add herbs, salt/pepper to meat while cooking.
  5. Once noodles & meatballs/beef is cooked serve and top with graded cheese!
  6. ENJOY!
