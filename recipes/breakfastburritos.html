Breakfast burrito

Description

The ultimate breakfast experience is only moment away, loaded with your choice of meats/cheese/salsa & veggies! All items are made fresh and at the time you order which means its truly hot n ready!



Ingredients
  • Tortilla - corn or flour (reduced fat/carbs avail)
  • Sausage
  • Bacon
  • Carnitas
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Avocado
  • Sour Cream
  • Bell peppers
  • Potatoes





Instructions
  1. Lightly toast your tortilla on each side and add sour cream
  2. lay cheese inside tortilla and let toast on stove to melt
  3. Prepare eggs and load into burrito
  4. Add chopped bacon, sausage
  5. Add chopped potatoes and avocado
  6. Tightly roll burrito while avoiding the unforgivable tear!
  7. ENJOY!
