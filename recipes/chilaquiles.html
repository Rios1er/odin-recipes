Chilaquiles

Description

Chilaquiles are said to have been first brought to America in a cookbook by Encarnación Pinedo, “The Spanish Cook” in 1898


Ingredients
  • Simmering red or green salsa
  • Layers of shredded chicken strips
  • Avocado slices
  • Radishes
  • Chopped onions
  • Sprinkles of queso fresco
  • Drizzle of crema
  • eggs

Instructions
  1. Start by frying tortillas and drain after being fried.
  2. Make the salsa roja
  3. Place the tortillas down and layer with chicken, cheese, eggs and salsa.
  4. Enjoy your meal!
